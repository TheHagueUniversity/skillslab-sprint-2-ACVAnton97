What you need to know about FC Spartak Moscow

Spartak is a Russian football club from Moscow found on April 18th in 1922. It is one of the most popular football clubs in Russia, having won the Soviet Top League 12 times and the Russian Premier League 9 times. Nicknames of the club include: 'Red n' Whites', 'Spartachi', 'Meat' and 'People's Team'. It is currently ranked as the 101st best team in the world according to the UEFA ratings. The team's current budget is around $120,000,000.
